Welcome to you home page:

Administrator Dashboard

Here we have 4 panels, each will send you to another place


Mange Employees

Here it will be able to create a user.
You will have to register each and every employee that you have.
You as admin should create their account and will notify them later with an email.
Here you can create, edit and delete employees.

Search for an employee

You can search for an employee by their name, email, or phone number.
Please make sure you have all emplyees selected in the dropdown menu. If you don't you will not be able to search for all employees.


Manage Requests

Employees will be able to send you request for PTO and sick days.
You will able to see who request and accept or deny their request

Create Publications

Here you will have the ability to compose your publication.
You have your title, your content, and the ability to upload an images.

In this section, you will be able to create, edit and delete publications you make.

View Calendar

In the view calendar section you’ll be able to view a an overview of all the absent employees and publications made.

Here you will have a much more visual understanding of your staff and your publications that you’ve made.

Feel free to use this when determining employees request for PTO or sick days.
